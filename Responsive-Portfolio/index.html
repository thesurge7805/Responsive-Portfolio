About Me

Born and raised in the southwest side of Chicago. After high school I did a 4 year enlistment with the United States Marine Corps. In the Marines, I deployed twice, one to Fallujah Iraq, and an expiditionary unit deployment which had me sail around southwest Asia and the Middle East on a Navy ship. After my discharge I moved to Las Vegas where I worked security at the Tropicana Hotel and Casino for around 1 Year. After that I enrolled in Roosevelt University to persue a criminal justice degree. Bouncing around various jobs and the boredom of bieng over-qualified for almost every job I have had has motivated me to take on the challenge of this Bootcamp.
